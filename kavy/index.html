Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

I provide services for the design and development of premium landing pages & websites. Supplementary services for design, animation and writing may also be provided based on project.

How much does a landing page cost?

I charge based on a fixed price, starting at $3000 per project. Project Costs can depend on factors like number of pages, integrations & functions needed.

What do you build your websites with?

I use Figma for the design stage and exclusively use Framer for the development. Both are industry-leading platforms that can cater to the needs of your project.

How long does a project take?

Projects, understandably, vary in length. An average project (single landing page) can take 1 or 2 weeks accounting for ideation, revisions etc.

What is the process for a project?

My process involves: 1. Ideation 2. Mood board 3. Wireframe 4. Design 5. Development
